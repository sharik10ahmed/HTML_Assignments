The Ultimate Midnight Chocolate Cake

Description:

This isn't just another chocolate cake—it is the moistest, fudgiest, most intense chocolate experience you will ever have. It uses boiling water to "bloom" the cocoa powder, releasing deep flavor notes that standard mixing just can't match. Best of all? You only need one bowl and a whisk.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions:

  1. Preheat and Prep: Heat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 8-inch round baking pans.
  2. Mix Dry Ingredients:In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add Wet Ingredients:Add the eggs, milk, and oil. Mix for about 2 minutes until smooth.
  4. The Secret Step:Carefully stir in the boiling water. The batter will be very thin and watery—this is normal!
  5. BakePour into pans and bake for 30–35 minutes. Cool completely before frosting.
